Biography

I’m a Research Assistant at National Chiao Tung University (NCTU) CS, recently graduated from NCTU with a Master’s degree in Computer Science. I’m now working with Prof. Wei-Chen Chiu.

I’m working on a few projects on image/video enhancement, including video frame interpolation and video super-resolution.

During my graduate studies, I worked on saliency-guided image manipulation and image enhancement under Prof. Wei-Chen Chiu and Dr. Yi-Hsuan Tsai.
